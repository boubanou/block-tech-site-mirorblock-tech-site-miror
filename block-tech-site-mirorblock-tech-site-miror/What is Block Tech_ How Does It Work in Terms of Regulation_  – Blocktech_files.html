What is Block Tech? How Does It Work in Terms of Regulation? 

Block Tech is revolutionizing the real estate industry by introducing a powerful platform that combines fractional real estate, PropTech innovations, crowdfunding, and royalties-based models. Understanding Block Tech: The Basics 

1. Technology Integration in Real Estate 

Block Tech is a cutting-edge PropTech platform that allows clients, investors, and businesses to explore the advantages of fractional real estate ownership. It simplifies complex real estate processes while providing transparency, scalability, and automation. 

 

Here’s how it works: 

  • Fractional Ownership: Investors can purchase fractional shares in real estate projects starting at low amounts like €10. 
  • Royalties-Based Models: Instead of traditional equity or debt crowdfunding, Block Tech offers a unique model where investors earn regular payouts tied to property revenues. 
  • Custom White-Label Solutions: Real estate professionals and startups can launch their own branded platforms using Block Tech’s customizable software. 
Key Features of Block Tech

Accessible Investments 
By breaking down high-value real estate into affordable shares, Block Tech democratizes property investment for everyone. 

 

Effortless Crowdfunding 
It supports multiple crowdfunding models: 

 

Equity Crowdfunding: Ownership shares in a property. 

 

Debt Crowdfunding: Lending to developers for fixed returns. 

 

Royalties Crowdfunding: Earnings tied to property performance. 

 

Regulatory Compliance 
Block Tech ensures all transactions are legally compliant, backed by strong legal opinions and alignment with local regulations. 

Navigating Regulations: How Block Tech Works 

One of the standout features of Block Tech is its ability to operate seamlessly within the complex regulatory frameworks governing real estate crowdfunding. Here’s how: 

1. Licensing and Compliance 
  • Block Tech aligns with real estate agency regulations, as royalties-based models often fall under this category. 

 

  • Each platform operates under specific licensing requirements tailored to the country or region. 
2. Legal Opinion for Every Platform 

Block Tech ensures each white-label platform has a legal opinion tailored to its business model and jurisdiction, enhancing investor trust and compliance. 

3. Contract-Based Transactions 

The platform relies on contractual agreements between property owners and investors, avoiding the complexities of securities regulations in many cases.

Block-Tech.co operates as a royalty-based crowdfunding software provider, specializing in fractional real estate investment. Our platform enables licensed real estate professionals to offer royalty rights to their clients via white-label solutions, focusing on real estate rather than traditional financial investments. Unlike platforms offering equity or loans, our model is not subject to financial regulations such as:

  • MiFID II (Directive 2014/65/EU): Royalty rights are not considered financial instruments under Article 4(1)(15) or Recital 27.
  • European Crowdfunding Service Providers Regulation (EU 2020/1503 – ECSP):Block-Tech.co’s revenue-sharing agreements are excluded from equity- or loan-based oversight under Article 3(1)(a).
  • Provision of Services for Financial Products (PSFP): Royalty rights do not qualify as securities under EU and French regulations, ensuring exemption from related financial rules.

Block-Tech.co and its white-label platforms operate under common contractual and consumer law. Clients must hold valid real estate agent licenses compliant with Directive 2005/36/EC, ensuring legal operation, liability insurance, and secure fund management in their jurisdictions. Since royalty rights do not qualify as financial instruments, neither Block-Tech.co nor its clients are required to submit a prospectus to ESMA or comply with MiFID II regulations related to securities or debt instruments.
